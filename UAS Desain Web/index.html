NieR:Automata

NieR:Automata is an action role-playing game developed by PlatinumGames and published by Square Enix. It was released in 2017 for PlayStation 4, Microsoft Windows, and later for Xbox One and Nintendo Switch.

Gameplay

The game combines action-based combat with role-playing elements. Players can switch between using melee and ranged attacks, and can evade enemy attacks through dodging and using a temporary barrier. The game features multiple endings and requires several playthroughs to fully experience the story.

Story

Set in the year 11945 AD, the story follows the battles of android soldiers 2B, 9S, and A2 in a proxy war between machines created by alien invaders and human-crafted androids. The story requires multiple playthroughs to fully unfold, each revealing new perspectives and plot elements.

Characters

  • 2B: The main protagonist, a combat android with a calm and collected personality.
  • 9S: A scanner type android who serves as 2B's partner.
  • A2: A prototype android who plays a significant role in later parts of the story.

Reception

NieR:Automata received critical acclaim for its story, gameplay, music, and themes. It has sold over 7 million copies worldwide and has been praised for its philosophical depth and unique narrative structure.
