YoRHa A2

A2, short for YoRHa Type A No.2, is a rogue YoRHa android and one of the main protagonists in NieR:Automata.

Appearance

A2 has long, silver hair and pale skin. Her YoRHa uniform is heavily damaged, revealing parts of her android frame. She doesn't wear the standard YoRHa visor.

Personality

A2 is initially portrayed as cold, stoic, and misanthropic. She harbors a deep hatred for machines and is driven by a desire for revenge. However, as her story unfolds, she reveals a more complex and empathetic side.

Abilities

A2 is an exceptional combatant, specializing in close-quarters combat. She can enter a unique Berserk Mode, sacrificing her health for increased damage output. Like other YoRHa units, she's skilled in hacking and using various weapons.
